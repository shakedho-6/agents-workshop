Change your mindset

Forget everything you know

Software used to be deterministic. You click a button, you get a fixed result. Agents are probabilistic. They don't just execute menus; they execute intent.

This is not plug-and-play

It's plug, train, play, teach, learn, unplug, and plug again. It's an iterative relationship, not a one-time setup.

Manage them like a new hire

You don't configure them—you onboard them. Give them clear context, set boundaries, and expect them to ask questions when they get stuck.
