Giving your agent its own WhatsApp number

Keep your personal number safe and let your agent run wild.

What you need

  • ✓ Numero app (App Store)
  • ✓ WhatsApp Business (Free)

Steps

  1. 1
    Download Numero and buy a WhatsApp-compatible number.
    Expect to pay ~$20 for a full year.
  2. 2
    Download WhatsApp Business and sign up using your new Numero number.
  3. 3
    When asked to verify, choose SMS, open the Numero app, and copy the code.
  4. 4
    Done. Your agent now has its own number.

💡 Heads up: Since March 2026, WhatsApp has gradually started rolling out dual-account support for regular WhatsApp on iOS. Check Settings > Account to see if you can add a second number directly before downloading the Business app!

⚠️ Important: When connecting your agent in Ocana, make sure you scan the QR code using this new WhatsApp Business account, not your personal one.

What you need

  • ✓ Numero app (Google Play)
  • ✓ Your existing WhatsApp app

Steps

  1. 1
    Download Numero and buy a WhatsApp-compatible number.
    Expect to pay ~$20 for a full year.
  2. 2
    Open your existing WhatsApp, go to Settings > Account > Add account.
    Android natively lets you run two numbers on the same app.
  3. 3
    Enter your new Numero number.
  4. 4
    When asked to verify, choose SMS, open the Numero app, and copy the verification code.
  5. 5
    Done. You can now easily switch between your personal and agent profiles.

⚠️ Important: When connecting your agent in Ocana, make sure you switch to the agent's account profile and scan the QR code from there.
